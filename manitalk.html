ManiTalk · Android

Your payments,
out loud.

Your payments, out loud.

When you're running a market stall or street food cart, you can't always see your phone. ManiTalk listens for Thai bank payment SMS confirmations and reads them aloud — in Thai and English — so you always know the money arrived.

100% Free — no subscription, no ads
Get it on Google Play
Features

Hear your money
arrive.

Designed for street vendors, market stalls, and anyone who accepts QR payments in Thailand — ManiTalk does one thing and does it perfectly.

🔊
Read Aloud
Payment confirmations are read aloud immediately when the SMS arrives. Hear the amount and sender bank in Thai and English.
📱
QR Payments
Works with all Thai QR code payment systems — PromptPay, QR30, and bank-specific QR codes. Any amount, instantly announced.
🇹🇭
Thai & English
Announces payments in both Thai and English. Works for Thai vendors and foreign tourists paying at your stall.
⚡
Instant & Offline
Reads SMS directly — no internet required for detection. Works even with poor signal. No server, no delay.
🔒
Privacy First
Processes SMS locally on device. No data leaves your phone. No account required. No tracking, no analytics.
🎁
Completely Free
No subscription, no ads, no in-app purchases. ManiTalk is free because street vendors shouldn't pay to hear their own money.

All major Thai
banks supported.

How It Works

Three steps,
zero setup.

01
Install & Grant Permission
Install ManiTalk from Google Play and grant SMS read permission. That's the only permission required.
02
Customer Scans & Pays
Your customer scans your PromptPay QR code and completes the payment through their bank app.
03
ManiTalk Reads It Aloud
The confirmation SMS arrives and ManiTalk immediately announces the amount and bank — in Thai and English. You heard the money arrive.
