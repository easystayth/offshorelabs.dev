Never miss a deadline in Thailand.

Thailand's visa rules are complex, change often, and the penalties for missing deadlines are severe. EasyStay TH keeps track of everything so you don’t have to — 90-day reports, visa extensions, TM-30 filings, all in one place, in your language.

Download on the App Store
Features

Everything Thailand
immigration demands.

From the 90-day report to the TM-30 landlord registration — EasyStay TH tracks every obligation so you stay legal and stress-free.

📅
90-Day Reports
Automatic countdown to your next 90-day report. Push notifications 30, 7, and 1 day before the deadline. Never miss a report date again.
📄
Visa Extensions
Track visa expiry dates for all major visa types. Get reminders before your visa expires and know exactly which immigration office to visit.
🏠
TM-30 Filing
Track TM-30 landlord registration requirements. Know when you need to re-register after returning from a trip. Required by Thai law.
👪
Family Mode
Track deadlines for your entire family in one app. Each family member gets their own profile with separate visa types and deadlines.
🌙
Forced Dark Mode
Designed for the night — dark-first UI that looks great in any light. Frosted glass interface with clean, readable typography.
🌐
7 Languages
Full interface in EN, RU, DE, FR, ES, IT, and UK. Because most Thailand expats don’t speak Thai, and immigration forms don’t speak English.
